# Chapter 1: General Principles / 第一章：通用原则

> These five principles apply not just to quant interviews — they are universal habits for structured problem-solving under pressure.  
> 这五条原则不只适用于量化面试，而是在压力下进行结构化解题的普遍习惯。

---

## 1. Build a Broad Knowledge Base / 建立广泛的知识体系

### 🇬🇧 English

Quant interviews can cover a wide range of topics — brain teasers, calculus, linear algebra, probability, stochastic processes, finance, and programming. Interviewers come from diverse backgrounds and tend to ask questions related to their own expertise, which is why breadth matters.

**Two key reasons why broad knowledge helps:**
- Interviewers have different specialties; you don't know which topic will come up
- Broad knowledge improves your problem-solving ability — it helps you see connections and alternative approaches

> ⚠️ **Key insight**: The goal is *basic understanding*, not expert-level mastery. Know the fundamentals very well. Most textbooks referenced in this field have "introduction" or "first" in their title for a reason.

### 🇨🇳 中文

量化面试可能涵盖广泛的主题——脑筋急转弯、微积分、线性代数、概率论、随机过程、金融和编程。面试官来自不同背景，倾向于问自己擅长领域的问题，这就是为什么知识面的广度很重要。

**广泛知识有帮助的两个关键原因：**
- 面试官各有专长，你无法预知会考哪个方向
- 广博的知识能提升解题能力——帮助你发现联系，找到替代思路

> ⚠️ **核心要点**：目标是"基本理解"，而非专家级掌握。把基础知识学扎实是最重要的。

---

## 2. Practice Your Interview Skills / 练习面试技巧

### 🇬🇧 English

The interview process starts long before you enter the room. Many behavioral, technical, and resume-related questions can be anticipated. Practice does not mean memorizing answers — it means building the mental fluency to reason quickly and communicate clearly under pressure.

**What to practise:**
- Common brain teaser patterns (weighing, probability, logic)
- STAR-format behavioral answers
- Explaining your thinking out loud while solving problems

> 💡 **Remember**: Just memorizing canned answers is dangerous. Experienced interviewers are very good at detecting this. Focus on understanding the *why* behind each solution.

### 🇨🇳 中文

面试准备在你走进房间之前就已经开始了。很多行为类、技术类和简历相关的问题都是可以预判的。练习不是死记硬背答案，而是培养在压力下快速推理、清晰表达的思维敏捷度。

**应该练习的内容：**
- 常见脑筋急转弯类型（称重、概率、逻辑）
- STAR格式的行为类回答
- 解题过程中大声说出自己的思路

> 💡 **记住**：死记硬背现成答案是危险的。有经验的面试官非常善于识别这一点。重点是理解每个解法背后的"为什么"。

## 3. Listen Carefully / 仔细倾听

### 🇬🇧 English

Be an active listener. If any part of a problem is unclear, politely ask for clarification. For multi-sentence problems, jot down key words. Interviewers often embed clues in how they frame the problem — even the assumptions they provide can hint at the approach.

**Practical tips:**
- Write down key information as the problem is stated
- Do not rush to answer — make sure you understand the full question first
- Listen for assumptions — they often point to the solution method

### 🇨🇳 中文

要做一个主动的倾听者。如果问题的任何部分不清楚，礼貌地请求澄清。对于多句话的问题，记下关键词。面试官在陈述问题时通常会嵌入线索——就连他们提供的假设条件，也往往暗示了解题方向。

**实用技巧：**
- 题目陈述时随手记录关键信息
- 不要急于回答——先确保完全理解题目
- 留意假设条件——它们往往指向解法

---

## 4. Speak Your Mind / 大声说出你的思路

### 🇬🇧 English

Never analyze silently. Verbalize your reasoning at every step. This is one of the most important skills in any quantitative interview.

**Why this matters:**
- It shows the interviewer your thought process, not just your answer
- If you go off-track, the interviewer can redirect you
- Interviewers are often testing a *specific concept* — speaking your mind shows them you understand it

> 🎯 **Practical rule**: If a conclusion is obvious to you, state it briefly without every detail. Focus your verbal explanation on the *key insight* of each problem.

### 🇨🇳 中文

永远不要默默分析。在每个步骤都要口头表达你的推理过程。这是量化面试中最重要的技能之一。

**为什么这很重要：**
- 它向面试官展示的是你的思考过程，而不仅仅是答案
- 如果你偏离了方向，面试官可以纠正你
- 面试官通常在考察某个*特定概念*——说出思路能证明你真正理解了

> 🎯 **实用规则**：如果某个结论对你来说显而易见，简短陈述即可，不必逐步说明。将口头解释聚焦在每道题的*核心洞察*上。

---

## 5. Make Reasonable Assumptions / 做出合理假设

### 🇬🇧 English

In real work and in interviews, you will often lack complete information. When the problem is ambiguous or under-specified, make your own assumptions — but **explain them clearly** to the interviewer so you get immediate feedback.

**The keyword is *reasonable*:**
- Base assumptions on common sense and the problem context
- State assumptions before using them
- Be ready to revise if the interviewer pushes back

> ✅ This skill directly mirrors what quants do in practice: build models with imperfect data by making defensible assumptions.

### 🇨🇳 中文

在实际工作和面试中，你往往无法获得完整的信息。当问题模糊或条件不足时，自行做出假设——但要**清楚地向面试官说明**，以便获得及时反馈。

**关键词是*合理*：**
- 基于常识和题目背景做出假设
- 在使用假设之前先声明
- 准备好在面试官提出质疑时进行修正

> ✅ 这项技能直接反映了量化分析师在实践中的工作方式：在数据不完整的情况下，通过可辩护的假设建立模型。

# Chapter 2: Brain Teasers / 第二章：脑筋急转弯

> Brain teasers require only common sense, logic, and at most high school level math. But they are not easy — they test **analytical thinking**, **creativity**, and **structured reasoning**.  
> 脑筋急转弯只需要常识、逻辑和最多高中水平的数学。但它们并不简单——它们考验的是**分析思维**、**创造力**和**结构化推理**。

---

## 2.1 Problem Simplification / 问题简化

### 🔑 Core Concept / 核心概念

**🇬🇧** When a problem seems overwhelming, reduce it to its simplest form first. Start with n=1 or n=2, solve that, then gradually increase complexity. A pattern almost always emerges that guides the full solution.

**🇨🇳** 当问题看起来难以下手时，先将其简化为最简单的形式。从 n=1 或 n=2 开始，解决之后再逐步增加复杂度。几乎总会出现一个规律，引导你得到完整解答。

---

### 🏴‍☠️ Problem 1: Screwy Pirates / 海盗分金币

**Question / 题目:**  
Five pirates looted 100 gold coins. The most senior pirate will propose a distribution of the coins. All pirates, including the
most senior pirate, will then vote. If at least 50% of the pirates (3 pirates in this case)
accept the proposal, the gold is divided as proposed. If not, the most senior pirate will be
fed to shark and the process starts over with the next most senior pirate ... The process is
repeated until a plan is approved. You can assume that all pirates are perfectly rational:
Pirates prioritize: (1) survival, (2) maximum gold, (3) fewest pirates on boat. How will the gold coins be divided in the end?

五个海盗抢了100枚金币。由资历最高的海盗提出分配方案并投票。若≥50%同意，按方案分配；否则该海盗被扔给鲨鱼，下一个海盗接管。海盗的优先级：(1)活命，(2)金币最多，(3)船上人越少越好。

**Solution / 解答:**

| Pirates (n) / 海盗数 | Senior Pirate's Strategy / 资历最高的海盗策略 |
|---|---|
| 1 | Takes all 100 coins / 拿走全部100枚 |
| 2 | Senior keeps 100, gives 0 to Pirate 1 / 全拿 |
| 3 | Gives Pirate 1 one coin (who gets 0 if 3 is killed) / 给海盗1一枚 |
| 4 | Gives Pirate 2 one coin (who gets 0 if 4 is killed) / 给海盗2一枚 |
| **5** | **Gives Pirates 1 and 3 one coin each, keeps 98 / 给1和3各一枚，自留98枚** |

**Pattern / 规律:** For any (2n+1) pirate case, the most senior pirate gives 1 coin each to pirates 1, 3, 5, ..., 2n-1 and keeps the rest.  
对于任意(2n+1)个海盗的情况，资历最高的海盗给海盗1、3、5...2n-1各一枚金币，其余自留。

> 💡 **Key Insight / 关键洞察**: Always start with the smallest case and build up. The "who gets nothing in the reduced case" logic drives the entire solution.  
> 永远从最小情况开始逐步推导。"在简化情形中谁什么都得不到"这一逻辑驱动了整个解答。

---

### 🐯 Problem 2: Tiger and Sheep / 老虎与羊

**Question / 题目:**  
100 tigers and 1 sheep on an island with grass. A tiger that eats the sheep becomes a sheep. All tigers are rational and want to survive. Will the sheep be eaten?

100只老虎和1只羊在一个只有草的岛上。吃了羊的老虎会变成羊。所有老虎都是理性的，且想要存活。羊会被吃掉吗？

**Solution / 解答:**

| Tigers (n) / 老虎数 | Outcome / 结果 | Reason / 原因 |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Sheep eaten / 羊被吃 | No risk to the tiger / 老虎没有风险 |
| 2 | Sheep safe / 羊安全 | If either eats, it becomes a sheep and gets eaten / 吃了就变成羊被吃掉 |
| 3 | Sheep eaten / 羊被吃 | After eating, 2 tigers remain → safe (same as n=2 case) / 剩2只老虎→安全 |
| 4 | Sheep safe / 羊安全 | After eating, 3 tigers remain → sheep will be eaten / 剩3只→羊会被吃 |
| ... | ... | ... |
| **100 (even)** | **Sheep safe / 羊安全** | **Even number → safe / 偶数→安全** |

**Rule / 规律:** If n is **even** → sheep is safe. If n is **odd** → sheep is eaten.  
若 n 为**偶数** → 羊安全。若 n 为**奇数** → 羊被吃。

### 🔑 Core Concept / 核心概念

**🇬🇧** These problems require step-by-step deduction. Each piece of information eliminates possibilities until only one conclusion remains. Write down what each statement implies.

**🇨🇳** 这类问题需要逐步推导。每条信息都能排除一些可能性，直到只剩一个结论。写下每条陈述的含义。

### 🌊 Problem 3: River Crossing / 过河问题

**Question / 题目:**  
A, B, C, D need to cross a river. Max 2 per crossing. Only 1 torch. Speed of pair = slower person. Times: A=10min, B=5min, C=2min, D=1min. Minimum time?

A、B、C、D需要过河。每次最多2人，只有1支火炬，速度取较慢者。各自用时：A=10分钟，B=5分钟，C=2分钟，D=1分钟。最少需要多少分钟？

**Solution / 解答:**

| Step / 步骤 | Action / 动作 | Time / 时间 |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | C & D cross → C & D过河 | 2 min |
| 2 | D returns → D返回 | 1 min |
| 3 | A & B cross → A & B过河 | 10 min |
| 4 | C returns → C返回 | 2 min |
| 5 | C & D cross → C & D过河 | 2 min |
| **Total / 合计** | | **17 min / 17分钟** |

> 💡 **Key Insight / 关键洞察**: The 10-minute person must cross with the 5-minute person (not alone or with a fast person). The fast runners D and C must shuttle the torch.  
> 10分钟的人必须和5分钟的人一起过（不能单独或和快的人搭档）。快的人D和C负责护送火炬。

### 🎂 Problem 4: Birthday Problem / 生日问题

**Question / 题目:**  
Boss A's birthday is one of: Mar 4, Mar 5, Mar 8 / Jun 4, Jun 7 / Sep 1, Sep 5 / Dec 1, Dec 2, Dec 8. You know the month; colleague C knows the day. You say: "I don't know A's birthday, and C doesn't either." C says: "I didn't know, but now I do." You say: "Now I know too." What is A's birthday?

老板A的生日是以下10个日期之一：3月4日、3月5日、3月8日、6月4日、6月7日、9月1日、9月5日、12月1日、12月2日、12月8日。你知道月份，同事C知道日期。你说："我不知道A的生日，C也不知道。"C说："我本来不知道，但现在知道了。"你说："我现在也知道了。"A的生日是哪天？

**Step-by-step Deduction / 逐步推导:**

```
Step 1: You say C doesn't know →
  The month cannot contain a unique day (2 or 7 are unique across all months)
  → Eliminate June (has day 7) and December (has day 2)
  → Remaining: March and September

第一步：你说C不知道 →
  月份不能包含唯一的日期（2和7在所有月份中是唯一的）
  → 排除六月（有7日）和十二月（有2日）
  → 剩余：三月和九月

Step 2: C now knows →
  After eliminating Jun/Dec, C can narrow it down to one date
  → The day must be unique in {Mar, Sep} list
  → Mar 5 and Sep 5 both appear → eliminate them
  → Remaining: Mar 4, Mar 8, Sep 1

第二步：C现在知道了 →
  排除六/十二月后，C能确定唯一日期
  → 该日在{三月、九月}列表中必须是唯一的
  → 3月5日和9月5日都出现了→排除
  → 剩余：3月4日、3月8日、9月1日

Step 3: You now know →
  You know the month. Mar 4 and Mar 8 are both in March → can't decide
  → Must be September → A's birthday = September 1

第三步：你现在也知道了 →
  你知道月份。3月4日和3月8日都在三月→无法确定
  → 必定是九月 → A的生日 = 9月1日
```

**Answer / 答案: September 1 / 9月1日**

### 🃏 Problem 5: Card Game / 纸牌游戏

**Question / 题目:**  
Casino game: Flip 2 cards at a time. Both black → dealer's pile. Both red → your pile. One of each → discard. If you have more cards, you win $100. How much would you pay to play?

赌场游戏：每次翻2张牌。两张黑→归庄家；两张红→归你；一红一黑→丢弃。如果你的牌更多，赢100美元。你愿意花多少钱玩这个游戏？

**Solution / 解答:**

Each discarded pair has exactly 1 red + 1 black card. Therefore, the number of red and black cards removed is always equal. This means the remaining red cards (your pile) always equals the remaining black cards (dealer's pile).

**The dealer always wins. Pay $0.**

每对被丢弃的牌恰好有1张红和1张黑。因此，被移除的红色和黑色牌数量始终相等。这意味着剩余的红色牌（你的牌堆）始终等于剩余的黑色牌（庄家的牌堆）。

**庄家永远赢。应支付0美元。**

> 💡 **Key Insight / 关键洞察**: Symmetry — if the discard process is balanced, the piles must always be equal. Use symmetry arguments before calculation.  
> 对称性——如果丢弃过程是平衡的，两堆牌必然始终相等。在计算之前先用对称性论证。

### 🔥 Problem 6: Burning Ropes / 燃烧的绳子

**Question / 题目:**  
Two ropes, each takes 1 hour to burn (non-uniform density). How do you measure exactly 45 minutes?

两根绳子，每根燃烧完需1小时（密度不均匀）。如何计量恰好45分钟？

**Solution / 解答:**

```
Action / 操作:
  t=0: Light BOTH ends of rope 1, and ONE end of rope 2
       同时点燃绳子1的两端，以及绳子2的一端

  t=30min: Rope 1 burns out (30 min, because both ends burn simultaneously)
           绳子1燃尽（两端同时燃烧，所以是30分钟）
           → Immediately light the OTHER end of rope 2
             立即点燃绳子2的另一端

  t=45min: Rope 2 burns out (it had 30min left, now burns from both ends = 15min)
           绳子2燃尽（还剩30分钟，两端燃烧=15分钟）

Total = 30 + 15 = 45 minutes / 合计 = 30 + 15 = 45分钟
```

### ⚖️ Problem 7: Defective Ball / 有缺陷的球

**Question / 题目:**  
12 identical balls, one is heavier OR lighter (unknown). Find the defective ball in 3 weighings using a balance scale.

12个看起来一样的球，其中一个比其余的重或轻（不知道哪种）。用天平在3次称量中找出有缺陷的球。

**Key Principle / 关键原理:**

Always divide into **3 groups** (not 2). Each weighing gives you information about the third group as well.

**总是分成3组**（而非2组）。每次称量也能给出关于第三组的信息。

**Strategy / 策略:**

```
Weighing 1: Weigh balls [1,2,3,4] vs [5,6,7,8]
称量1：称[1,2,3,4] vs [5,6,7,8]

Case A: They balance → defective ball is in [9,10,11,12]
情况A：平衡 → 有缺陷的球在[9,10,11,12]中

  Weighing 2: Weigh [9,10] vs [8,11] (8 is known normal)
  称量2：称[9,10] vs [8,11]（8是已知正常球）

    If 9,10 lighter → either 9,10 is Light, or 11 is Heavy
    如果9,10较轻 → 9或10是轻球，或11是重球

      Weighing 3: Weigh 9 vs 10
      称量3：称9 vs 10
      → 9<10: ball 9 is defective (light) / 9<10：9号球有缺陷（轻）
      → 9=10: ball 11 is defective (heavy) / 9=10：11号球有缺陷（重）
      → 9>10: ball 10 is defective (light) / 9>10：10号球有缺陷（轻）

    If 9,10=8,11 → ball 12 is defective
    如果9,10=8,11 → 12号球有缺陷

Case B: [1,2,3,4] < [5,6,7,8] → some of 1-4 are light OR some of 5-8 are heavy
情况B：[1,2,3,4] < [5,6,7,8] → 1-4中有轻球 或 5-8中有重球
(follow tree diagram in book for full solution)
（按书中树状图继续解答）
```

**General Rule / 通用规则:**
- With info on whether heavier/lighter: identify defective ball among **3ⁿ** balls in n weighings
- Without that info: identify among **(3ⁿ - 3)/2** balls in n weighings

### 🐎 Problem 8: Trailing Zeros / 末尾零个数

**Question / 题目:** How many trailing zeros in 100! ?  100!的末尾有多少个零？

**Solution / 解答:**

Trailing zeros come from factors of 10 = 2 × 5. Since factors of 2 far outnumber factors of 5, count the fives:  
末尾零来自10的因子 = 2 × 5。由于2的因子远多于5的因子，计算5的出现次数：

```
Numbers 1-100 divisible by 5:   20 numbers (5,10,...,100) → contributes 20 fives
Numbers 1-100 divisible by 25:   4 numbers (25,50,75,100) → contributes extra 4 fives

1-100中能被5整除的数：20个（5,10,...,100）→贡献20个5
1-100中能被25整除的数：4个（25,50,75,100）→额外贡献4个5

Total fives = 20 + 4 = 24
Answer: 24 trailing zeros / 答案：24个末尾零
```

### 🏇 Problem 9: Horse Race / 赛马问题

**Question / 题目:**  
25 horses, find the 3 fastest. Only 5 can race at once. Minimum number of races?

25匹马，找出最快的3匹。每次最多5匹参赛。最少需要几场比赛？

**Solution / 解答:**

```
Round 1 (5 races): Race all horses in 5 groups of 5
第一轮（5场）：将所有马分为5组，每组5匹分别比赛

Round 2 (1 race): Race the 5 group winners → establishes overall ranking
第二轮（1场）：让5个小组的冠军互相比赛→确定总体排名

  After Round 2, assume winners rank: W1 > W2 > W3 > W4 > W5
  第二轮后，假设冠军排名：W1 > W2 > W3 > W4 > W5
```

### 🔑 Core Concept / 核心概念

**🇬🇧** `x mod y` (written x%y) is the remainder when x is divided by y. Key property: if x₁%y = x₂%y, then (x₁-x₂) is divisible by y. Modular arithmetic is powerful for problems involving cycles, remainders, and color/category patterns.

**🇨🇳** `x mod y`（写作x%y）是x除以y的余数。关键性质：如果x₁%y = x₂%y，则(x₁-x₂)能被y整除。模运算对于涉及循环、余数和颜色/类别模式的问题非常强大。

### 👒 Problem 29: Prisoner Problem / 囚犯问题

**Question / 题目:**  
100 prisoners, each gets a red or blue hat. Each can see all others' hats but not their own. Called out one by one, each guesses their hat color out loud. Everyone hears. Maximize survivors.

100名囚犯，每人戴红或蓝帽子。每人能看到其他所有人的帽子，但看不到自己的。被依次叫出，每人大声猜自己的帽子颜色，其他人都能听到。最大化生还人数。

**Solution / 解答:**

```
Strategy / 策略:
Prisoner 1 (sacrificial) declares RED if he sees an ODD number of red hats
第1名囚犯（牺牲者）：若看到奇数顶红帽则宣布"红色"

Every subsequent prisoner can deduce their own hat color:
之后每名囚犯都能推断出自己的帽子颜色：
- They know the parity (odd/even) announced by Prisoner 1
  他们知道第1人宣布的奇偶性
- They can count the red hats they see themselves
  他们能自己数看到的红帽数量
- Together → they know their own hat color
  结合起来→知道自己的帽子颜色

Guarantee: 99 saved, Prisoner 1 has 50% survival chance
保证：99人获救，第1名囚犯有50%的生还概率

Extension to 3 colors / 扩展到3种颜色:
Assign: Red=0, Green=1, Blue=2
赋值：红=0，绿=1，蓝=2
Prisoner 1 announces (total score of others)%3
第1名囚犯宣布（其他人总分）%3
Still guarantees 99 saved / 仍然保证99人获救
```

### ÷9 Problem 30: Division by 9 / 被9整除

**Rule / 规则:** An integer is divisible by 9 if and only if the **sum of its digits** is divisible by 9.  
一个整数能被9整除当且仅当其**各位数字之和**能被9整除。

**Proof sketch / 证明思路:** Every 10ᵏ - 1 = 999...9 is divisible by 9. So any number minus its digit sum is divisible by 9.  
每个10ᵏ - 1 = 999...9都能被9整除。所以任何数减去其数字之和能被9整除。

### 🦎 Problem 31: Chameleon Colors / 变色龙颜色

**Question / 题目:**  
Island: 13 red, 15 green, 17 blue chameleons. When two different-color chameleons meet, both change to the third color. Can all chameleons become the same color?

岛上：13只红色、15只绿色、17只蓝色变色龙。两只不同颜色的变色龙相遇时，都变成第三种颜色。所有变色龙能变成同一种颜色吗？

**Solution / 解答:**

```
Check counts mod 3 / 检查数量模3:
  Red:   13 = 3×4 + 1  → 13%3 = 1
  Green: 15 = 3×5 + 0  → 15%3 = 0  
  Blue:  17 = 3×5 + 2  → 17%3 = 2

When any two chameleons of different colors meet:
当任意两只不同颜色的变色龙相遇时：
  The relative remainders (mod 3) shift but never equalize
  相对余数（模3）会改变但永远不会相等

For all to become one color, at some point two colors must have equal count
要使所有变色龙变成同一颜色，某时刻必须有两种颜色数量相等
→ Their counts%3 must be equal
  → 它们的数量%3必须相等

Since 1, 0, and 2 are all different (mod 3), and the pattern is preserved,
two colors can NEVER have the same count mod 3.
由于1、0和2（模3）都不同，且这种模式被保持，
两种颜色永远不能有相同的数量（模3）。

Answer: IMPOSSIBLE / 答案：不可能
```

## 2.8 Math Induction / 数学归纳法

### 🔑 Core Concept / 核心概念

**Steps of Proof by Induction / 数学归纳法的步骤:**

1. **Base case / 基础步骤**: Prove P(1) (or smallest valid n)
2. **Inductive step / 归纳步骤**: Prove that P(n) → P(n+1) for all n
3. **Conclusion / 结论**: P(n) is true for all valid n

> 💡 The hardest part is usually identifying the right predicate P(n). Simplify the problem first to find the pattern.  
> 最难的部分通常是找到正确的谓词P(n)。先简化问题以找到规律。

### 🪙 Problem 32: Coin Split Problem / 硬币分割问题

**Question / 题目:**  
Split 1000 coins into two piles, multiply sizes (xy). Keep splitting and adding products. What is the final sum, regardless of how you split?

将1000枚硬币分成两堆，将数量相乘（xy）。继续分割并累加乘积，直到所有堆只有1枚。无论如何分割，最终总和是多少？

**Solution / 解答:**

```
Pattern from small cases / 从小情况中发现规律:
f(2) = 1×1 = 1
f(3) = 1×2 + 1×1 = 3
f(4) = either 2×2 + f(2) + f(2) = 4+1+1 = 6
       or 1×3 + f(1) + f(3) = 3+0+3 = 6

Pattern: f(n) = n(n-1)/2
规律：f(n) = n(n-1)/2

For n = 1000: f(1000) = 1000 × 999 / 2 = 499,500
```

**Proof by strong induction / 强归纳法证明:**  
If f(k) = k(k-1)/2 for all k < N, then f(N) = x(N-x) + f(x) + f(N-x) = N(N-1)/2 ✓

### 🍫 Problem 33: Chocolate Bar / 巧克力棒

**Question / 题目:**  
Break a 6×8 chocolate bar into 48 individual squares. Each break splits one rectangle into two. How many total breaks?

将6×8的巧克力棒掰成48块单独的正方形。每次掰断将一个矩形分成两个。共需要多少次？

**Solution / 解答:**

```
General formula / 通用公式: m×n bar requires mn - 1 breaks
m×n的巧克力棒需要mn - 1次掰断

Intuitive proof / 直觉证明:
Start with 1 piece, end with mn pieces
从1块开始，以mn块结束
Each break adds exactly 1 piece
每次掰断恰好增加1块
Therefore: mn - 1 breaks needed
因此：需要mn - 1次掰断

For 6×8: 48 - 1 = 47 breaks / 对于6×8：需要47次
```

### 🏁 Problem 34: Race Track / 赛道问题

**Question / 题目:**  
Circular track, N gas cans with total fuel for exactly one lap. Car starts empty. Show there always exists a starting point to complete the full circle.

圆形赛道，N个油罐的总燃料恰好够跑一圈。汽车初始没油。证明总存在一个起点能完成完整一圈。

**Solution / 解答(Induction / 归纳法):**

```
Base case N=1: Trivially start at the gas can / 基础步骤N=1：从油罐位置出发即可

Inductive step / 归纳步骤:
For N+1 cans: there must exist at least one can i where
对于N+1个油罐：必定存在至少一个油罐i，使得
  (gas in can i) ≥ (distance to next can)
  （第i个油罐的油量）≥（到下一个油罐的距离）

Combine can i and can i+1 into one → reduces to N-can problem
将第i和第i+1个油罐合并为一个→化归为N个油罐的问题

By induction hypothesis, solution exists for N → exists for N+1
由归纳假设，N个油罐有解→N+1个油罐有解
```

## 2.9 Proof by Contradiction / 反证法

### 🔑 Core Concept / 核心概念

**🇬🇧** Assume the opposite of what you want to prove. Then show this assumption leads to a logical contradiction. Therefore, the original statement must be true.

**🇨🇳** 假设你想证明的命题的反面成立。然后证明这个假设会导致逻辑矛盾。因此，原命题必定为真。

### √ Problem 35: Irrational Number / 无理数

**Question / 题目:** Prove that √2 is irrational.  证明√2是无理数。

**Proof / 证明:**

```
Assume √2 = m/n (irreducible fraction, no common factors)
假设√2 = m/n（最简分数，没有公因子）

Then m² = 2n² → m² is even → m is even → m = 2k
则m² = 2n² → m²是偶数 → m是偶数 → m = 2k

Substituting: 4k² = 2n² → n² = 2k² → n is even
代入：4k² = 2n² → n² = 2k² → n是偶数

But both m and n even CONTRADICTS "no common factors"
但m和n都是偶数与"没有公因子"矛盾

Therefore √2 cannot be rational → √2 is irrational ✓
因此√2不能是有理数→√2是无理数 ✓
```

### 🌈 Problem 36: Rainbow Hats / 彩虹帽子

**Question / 题目:**  
7 prisoners, each gets a hat in one of 7 rainbow colors (executioner's choice). Each sees others' hats but not their own. Each writes a guess. If ANY ONE guesses correctly, all go free. Is there a guaranteed strategy?

7名囚犯，每人戴一顶7种彩虹颜色之一的帽子（由刽子手决定）。每人能看到他人的帽子，但看不到自己的。每人写下猜测。如果**任意一人**猜对，所有人获释。有保证成功的策略吗？

**Solution / 解答:**

```
Code colors 0-6, label prisoners 0-6
将颜色编码为0-6，囚犯也标记为0-6

Prisoner i's strategy / 第i名囚犯的策略:
Guess the color code g_i such that:
猜测颜色编码g_i使得：
  (g_i + sum of all others' color codes) % 7 = i

Proof that at least one guesses correctly (by contradiction) / 
证明至少一人猜对（用反证法）:

If NONE guess correctly, then for all i:
如果没有人猜对，则对所有i：
  (sum of all codes) % 7 ≠ i for i = 0,1,2,3,4,5,6

But (sum of all codes)%7 must be exactly ONE of {0,1,2,3,4,5,6}
但（所有编码之和）%7必定恰好是{0,1,2,3,4,5,6}中的一个

CONTRADICTION → at least one prisoner must guess correctly
矛盾→至少一名囚犯必定猜对

Answer: YES, the strategy guarantees freedom / 答案：是的，该策略保证获释
```
